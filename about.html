Health Information Management (HIM) Student Competition

Health Information Management (HIM) Student Challenge is the inaugural international competition organized by the International Federation of Health Information Management Association South East Asia-Region (IFHIMA SEAR). The purpose of this competition is to foster students' creativity and innovation in supporting the advancement of knowledge in the field of Health Information Management. This competition is open to students from all over the world.

Competition Flow

  • Pre-Registration

    Each team must consist of a maximum of 3 members. Participants can read the competition terms in the Term of Reference (TOR) for the desired competition category.

  • Registration

    Each team must fill out the registration form and complete the payment before September 30, 2024. The registration fee of 100 USD per team must be paid at the time of registration.

  • Final Competition

    The top 3 teams for each category will be selected and invited to the final competition venue (the organizer will provide a half-day meeting package for 2 people per team).

  • Final Decisions

    Each competition will be selected by a judges, and the final selection will be based on their decision.
